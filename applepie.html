Apple pie recipe:

ingredients:

for crust

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 6–8 tbsp ice water

for filling

  • 6–7 cups apples, peeled, cored, and sliced (Granny Smith, Honeycrisp, or a mix)
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • ¼ cup light brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter (for dotting on top)

Instruction

for the crust

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, cold and cubed
  • 6–8 tbsp ice water

For the filling:

  • 6–7 cups apples, peeled, cored, and sliced (Granny Smith, Honeycrisp, or a mix)
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • ¼ cup light brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • ¼ tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter (for dotting on top)

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients: In a large bowl, whisk together 2 ½ cups flour and 1 tsp salt.
  2. Cut in butter: Add 1 cup cold cubed butter and work it into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. Add ice water: Gradually add 6–8 tbsp ice water, mixing gently until dough just comes together.
  4. Divide and chill: Split dough into two disks, wrap in plastic, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  5. Prepare apples: In a large bowl, toss apples with sugars, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, lemon juice, and vanilla extract.
  6. Preheat oven: Set oven to 425°F (220°C).
  7. Roll out bottom crust: Roll one dough disk into a 12-inch circle and place in a 9-inch pie dish.
  8. Add filling: Pour apple mixture into crust and dot with 2 tbsp butter.
  9. Roll out top crust: Roll out second disk and place over filling. Cut slits for steam vents or create a lattice top.
  10. Seal edges: Trim excess dough and crimp edges to seal.
  11. Bake: Bake at 425°F for 20 minutes, then reduce to 350°F (175°C) and bake 40–50 minutes more until crust is golden and filling bubbles.
  12. Cool: Let pie cool for at least 2 hours before serving.

Enjoy!
