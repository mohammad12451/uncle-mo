My name is Mohammad. I have been cooking for the last 20 years. I have always loved cooking since I was young. It was always a lively experience. I remember standing in the kitchen with my mom, watching her cook as I learned how some desserts were made. Sometimes, I would even note the recipes down somewhere so I could try them later myself. Thankfully, I had parents who were really supportive. My mom would always call me to help her, even when she didn’t really need help. She knew I loved cooking and wanted to help or even do it on my own. That’s how I came up with the idea of creating a website to inspire people to cook.
