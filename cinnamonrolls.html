Cinnamon Rolls recipe:

Ingredients:

For the dough:

  • 1 cup whole milk, warm (about 110°F / 43°C)
  • 2 ¼ tsp active dry yeast (1 packet)
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • ⅓ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 4 cups all-purpose flour

For the filling:

  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 3 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • ⅓ cup unsalted butter, softened

For the cream cheese frosting:

  • 4 oz cream cheese, softened
  • ¼ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 ½ cups powdered sugar
  • ½ teaspoon of vanilla extract

Instruction

Make the dough

  1. In a large bowl, combine the warm milk (110°F / 43°C) and active dry yeast. Let it sit for about 5–10 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  2. Add the sugar, melted butter, salt, and eggs to the yeast mixture. Stir until well combined.
  3. Gradually add the flour, 1 cup at a time, mixing until a soft dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough by hand (8–10 minutes) or with a mixer using a dough hook (5–7 minutes) until it's smooth and elastic.
  5. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean towel or plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 to 1.5 hours, or until doubled in size.

Prepare the filling

  1. In a small bowl, mix together the brown sugar and cinnamon.
  2. Keep the softened butter ready to spread on the dough later.

Roll and fill the dough

  1. Once risen, punch down the dough and turn it out onto a floured surface.
  2. Roll it into a rectangle, about 16 x 12 inches.
  3. Spread the softened butter evenly over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle the cinnamon-sugar mixture over the buttered dough.

Roll Up and Cut

  1. Starting from the long side, roll the dough tightly into a log.
  2. Cut into 12 even rolls (use a serrated knife or dental floss for clean cuts).

Second rise

  1. Place the rolls in a greased 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. Cover and let them rise for another 30–45 minutes, until puffy.

Bake

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Bake the rolls for 25–30 minutes, or until golden brown on top.

Make the Cream Cheese Frosting

  1. Beat together the cream cheese and butter until smooth.
  2. Add in the powdered sugar and vanilla extract, and beat until creamy.

Frost and serve

  1. Let the cinnamon rolls cool slightly, then spread the frosting over the warm rolls.
  2. Serve and enjoy!
