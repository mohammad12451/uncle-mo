Tiramisu recipe:

For the Cream Layer:

  • 6 large egg yolks

  • 1 pound (450g) mascarpone cheese, softened

Ingredients

For the Cream Layer:

  • 6 large egg yolks
  • ¾ cup (150g) granulated sugar
  • 1 cup (240ml) heavy cream
  • 1 pound (450g) mascarpone cheese, softened

For Assembly:

  • 1½ cups (360ml) strong brewed espresso or coffee, cooled
  • ¼ cup (60ml) coffee liqueur (optional)
  • 2 packs (7 oz or ~200g) ladyfinger biscuits (savoiardi)
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder (for dusting)
  • Dark chocolate shavings (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Whisk egg yolks and sugar together over a simmering pot of water for 8–10 minutes until thick and pale. Let cool.
  2. Whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form.
  3. Fold the mascarpone into the egg mixture, then gently fold in the whipped cream.
  4. Mix the cooled espresso with coffee liqueur (if using) in a shallow dish.
  5. Quickly dip ladyfingers into the coffee mixture and arrange in a 9x13-inch dish.
  6. Spread half of the cream mixture over the ladyfingers. Repeat layers.
  7. Refrigerate for at least 6 hours or overnight.
  8. Dust with cocoa powder and top with chocolate shavings before serving.
