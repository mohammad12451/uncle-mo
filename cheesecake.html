cheese cake recipes:

Ingredients:

For crust

  • 12 grams of chracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (melted)

For filling

  • 4 packages cream cheese
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs (room temperature)
  • 2 cups sour cream (optional, for a creamier filling)
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream (optional, for richness)

To do (tutorial)

Preheat oven to 325°F (163°C).

Make the crust:

  1. In a small bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter
  3. Mix well until everything is evenly coated.
  4. Press the mixture into the bottom of a springform pan (or a small pan of your choice) to form a compact crust.
  5. Bake the crust for about 10 minutes. Remove from the oven and set it aside to cool.

Prepare the filling:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese using an electric mixer on medium speed until smooth and creamy (about 2-3 minutes).
  2. Add the sugar and vanilla extract. Beat for another minute until combined.
  3. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  4. If using, add the sour cream and heavy cream for extra richness and creaminess. Beat until the mixture is smooth and velvety.

Assemble the cheesecake:

  1. Pour the cream cheese filling over the cooled crust, smoothing the top with a spatula.
  2. Gently tap the pan on the counter to release any air bubbles.

Bake:

  1. Place the cheesecake in the oven and bake for about 50-60 minutes. The center should still jiggle slightly when shaken, but the edges should be firm.
  2. Turn off the oven and let the cheesecake cool in the oven with the door cracked for about 1 hour to avoid cracks.

Chill:

After the cheesecake cools to room temperature, cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight for the best texture.

Serve:

  • Top with your favorite fruits, whipped cream, or a drizzle of chocolate if desired.
  • Slice and enjoy your delicious cheesecake!
